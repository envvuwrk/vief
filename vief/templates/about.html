About VFW

Vie Fashion Week (VFW) founded and created by Fashion Designers Akhil & Aiswarya, A Square London – The Fashion House. VFW is a very unique Fashion Week project by A Square London. Through VFW, the designer duo want to bring the world of fashion on to a single platform. They create the biggest, stunning, and innovative fashion runway experience in the world of fashion. Fashion Designers/Brands from across the globe showcase and exhibit their amazing creativity at VFW to enhance their brand visibility and sale. Global Designers at VFW gives the fashion world to witness the amalgamation of different Indigenous, international culture and latest trends on a single platform. It also brings the race, color and humanity together at VFW by saying “We don’t stand for Discrimination”. Designers, Boutiques, Textiles, Beauty Products, Accessories, Manufactures of Fabrics, Government and private entities will exhibit their latest Designs, Products, and Cutting Edge Technology to the world of fashion at VFW. Participants and guests can interact with Media, Celebrities, Experts, Bloggers, Vloggers, and Social Media Influencers to increase their business to a higher level. We bring the world of Fashion on to a single platform through VFW. We aim to be one of the top five fashion weeks in the world by 2030.

Founders and Creators
YOU ARE SPECIAL FOR US

Akhil & Aiswarya, launched their fashion organization, “A Square London” during their fashion studies in London in 2013. They have done numerous fashion projects in London, UAE, and in India. They are the first Indian fashion duo to do an Ancient Mural painting fashion show in London. In 2016, they did a fashion reality show for a national television broadcaster in India A Square London – launched the first edition of “Vie Fashion Week” in India with a star studded line up of International Designers, Models, Celebrities, International Beauty Pageants and Media from around the globe in association with the Kerala Tourism Department Corporation, India.

Through VFW, the designer duo want to bring the world of fashion on to a single platform.
